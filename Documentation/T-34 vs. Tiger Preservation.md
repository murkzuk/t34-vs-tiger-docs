This collection preserves the original 2008 tank simulation T-34 vs. Tiger, developed by G5 Software and published by Lighthouse Interactive. As both the original publisher and the physical activation servers are no longer operational, this upload serves as a historical archive of the game and the essential community-led ZeeWolf Mod

Included in this Archive:

    Original Game Media: A full digital preservation of the original game CD. https://archive.org/details/wwii-battle-tanks-t-34-vs-tiger-win-iso-en_202601

    ZeeWolf Mod: The classic community modification that expands the unit roster and mission depth. https://archive.org/details/t-34vs-tiger-zw-2015.7z
	
	Preservation Status: This material is provided for educational and historical purposes.
